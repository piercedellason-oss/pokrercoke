What is Poker?

About Poker

Poker is a strategic card game involving betting, bluffing, and hand rankings. Players compete to win chips by having the best hand or by convincing others to fold.

Basic Rules:

  • Each player gets cards.
  • Players place bets based on how strong they believe their hand is.
  • Players can fold, call, or raise.
  • The highest hand at the end wins the pot.
